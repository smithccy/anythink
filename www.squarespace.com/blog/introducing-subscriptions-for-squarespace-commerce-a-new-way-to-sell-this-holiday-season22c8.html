Introducing Subscriptions for Squarespace Commerce: A New Way To Sell This Holiday Season

November 15, 2018 Rafael Balbi

Whether your customers are shopping for others—or for themselves—this holiday season, we have the perfect gift solution for them: Squarespace Subscriptions. After a simple set up, you can sell your products on a recurring basis using our subscription feature. Whether you’re selling an ongoing monthly subscription box, an annual product delivery, or a temporary seasonal item, you can set up recurring and limited subscriptions that best meet your customers’ needs.

As soon as a customer purchases a subscription, we’ll start doing the work for you. No need to follow up — subsequent orders come in automatically. It has never been easier to build a loyal customer base for your brand.

With Subscriptions, there’s no need to learn about or pay for an additional service. Plus, you don’t have to worry about sending your customers through a third-party provider with different branding or confusing design. All transactions will take place directly on your Squarespace website so you can save time and money.

If you’re on the Commerce Advanced plan, you can start using the Subscriptions feature today. If not, it’s time to upgrade. If you have any questions, our Customer Care Team is available to help 24/7.
