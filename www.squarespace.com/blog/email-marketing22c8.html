Say More and Sell More with Squarespace Email Campaigns

June 12, 2018 Tim Miller

We’re pleased to announce the launch of Squarespace Email Campaigns, the newest addition to our growing suite of marketing tools. Connecting with your audience goes beyond your homepage, and now you can extend your brand to the emails you send your subscribers, driving engagement and increasing sales. Today, it’s easier than ever with Squarespace’s all-in-one platform.

Drafting an email campaign that mirrors the style and content of your website is simple: choose from dozens of customizable layouts to express what you have to say, whether you’re making an announcement, launching a product, or sharing other news. Our professionally designed layouts automatically pull in your logo and social icons so you can focus on your message. You can even populate them with existing content such as blog posts or products in one click, saving time and avoiding mistakes. And with Squarespace’s responsive designs, your emails will look great no matter the device they’re viewed on.

Squarespace Email Campaigns scales with you as your marketing endeavors grow, giving you the ability to target specific groups with unique mailing lists. Quickly understand how subscribers respond to your content with real-time analytics, such as open and click-through rates. Combined with the robust traffic and sales metrics in Squarespace Analytics, you now have a holistic view of how your audience engages with you and your brand.

Whether you’re promoting a sale for your online store, sharing your most recent blog post, or sending an invitation, Squarespace Email Campaigns helps you effectively connect with your audience. Existing Squarespace customers can use Email Campaigns at no cost for a limited time during an invite-only early access period. Customers will receive an email when it has been added to their account. General availability is coming soon at a low monthly rate.

Learn more about what Squarespace Email Campaigns can do for you and your business. As always, if you have any questions, our Customer Care team is here to help 24/7.
