Content creator and community maker Fran Tirado uses Squarespace as a hub for his online presence. As a writer, editor, podcast host, group organizer, and speaker proudly focusing on LGBTQ communities, Fran is able to showcase his wide range of interests in one beautiful place.
Visit imfran.com
Fran is the Executive Editor of Hello Mr. and has written on the lives of queer people for publications like Vice, Broadly, and BuzzFeed.
With mobile friendly designs, Fran’s site always looks great.
Whether Fran’s site visitors are at their desks or on the go like he is, they get an elegant view of Fran’s work. By featuring samples of his many past projects, Fran is able to immediately build credibility with his audience.
Integrating social accounts with his site is as simple as signing in.
Fran uses his website as a central hub for his online presence by including links to his social media accounts. This allows him to showcases his personality and engage with his audience over shared interests.
Fran tracks the success of his many projects with easy-to-understand metrics.
Fran is always juggling multiple side gigs, such as co-hosting a podcast called Food 4 Thot. Using Squarespace Analytics, he and his team stay up to date on traffic sources to the show’s website and strategize on how to find new listeners.
